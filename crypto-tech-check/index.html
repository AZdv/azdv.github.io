Crypto Technical Due Diligence

The ICOs Explosion

Since 2017, ICOs have been fueling the Crypto industry with the total funds surpassing the traditional VC market. As the numbers clearly show, 2018 is set to pass 8 Billion Dollars in ICO investment.

Investing in the right companies

While some Crypto companies are flourishing with promising tech, a large number of them pop and disappear like bubbles.
The biggest issue is that Crypto companies promise a lot, and end up not being able to deliver on their ideas.
Traditional investors can still have the upper hand, by demanding companies to go through a Crypto Technical Due Diligence.

Crypto Technical Due Diligence

Every Crypto company is different, some have a 2 page White Paper, some describe mathematical proof on a 30+ page document, some even have an alpha version of their technology ready to go. Our role is to be the technical team, working on the side of the investors. We work to bridge the technical gap, allowing the investor to know about the chances of these companies to implement a successful solution as promised.

Paper to Code

One of the key factors in our technical due diligence, is a process we call Paper to Code. We measure the feasibility of implementation for a company's White Paper into actual working software. Naturally the closer a company is to a public product, the higher the probability of a successful Paper to Code.
